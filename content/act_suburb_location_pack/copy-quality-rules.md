# Copy Quality Rules for Local SEO Pages

## Do

- Use suburb-specific region/type/context.
- Mention practical site considerations.
- Link to relevant services.
- Link to nearby suburbs.
- Keep copy concise and human.
- Include useful FAQs.
- Mention early feasibility, site access, orientation, slope, services, estate controls or established-block constraints where relevant.

## Do not

- Create duplicate pages where only the suburb name changes.
- Say "best builder" or unsupported claims.
- Invent awards, reviews, years of experience or project counts.
- Keyword stuff.
- Mention internal folders, placeholder content or implementation details.
- Give legal/planning advice.
- Promise approvals or outcomes.
