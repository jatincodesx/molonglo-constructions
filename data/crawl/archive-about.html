Our Story

Building dreams and transforming spaces in Canberra since 2019

Founded in 2019, Molonglo Construction Group has established itself as a trusted name in Canberra's residential construction industry. Our journey began with a vision to bring fresh perspectives and innovative solutions to home building while maintaining the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

"Bringing Dreams to Life"

More than just a slogan, it's our commitment to turning your vision into reality.

We've successfully completed more than 38 residential projects, from custom new homes , dual occupancy occupancies, terraces and multi unit development. Each project stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence and attention to detail.
